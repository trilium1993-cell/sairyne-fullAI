## Your Rights

Depending on your location (especially if you are in the EU), you have the following rights:

### **Access** (Art. 15 GDPR)
Right to access personal data we hold about you.

### **Rectification** (Art. 16 GDPR)
Right to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

### **Erasure** ("Right to be Forgotten") (Art. 17 GDPR)
Right to request deletion of personal data, with limited exceptions.

### **Restriction** (Art. 18 GDPR)
Right to restrict how we process your data.

### **Portability** (Art. 20 GDPR)
Right to receive your data in a structured, portable format.

### **Objection** (Art. 21 GDPR)
Right to object to processing for legitimate interests or marketing.

### **Withdraw Consent** (Art. 7(3) GDPR)
You can withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

### **Lodge a Complaint**
You have the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority (e.g., local privacy regulator).

### **How to Exercise Your Rights**

- Your name and email
- Specific right you're exercising

## Cookie Policy

### **What Are Cookies?**
Cookies are small text files placed on your device that help us recognize you and improve your experience.

### **Types of Cookies We Use**

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Duration |
|------------|---------|----------|
| **Essential** | Session management, security | Session |
| **Analytics** | Understand how users interact with Sairyne | 1-2 years |
| **Marketing** | Personalize content, ads (if applicable) | 1-2 years |
| **Preferences** | Remember your settings | 1 year |

### **Third-Party Cookies**
We may allow third parties (e.g., analytics providers) to place cookies. These are subject to their privacy policies.

### **Managing Cookies**
You can:
- Delete cookies in your browser settings
- Opt out of analytics via your browser's privacy settings
- Use "Do Not Track" (DNT) headers (we respect these)

**Note**: Disabling cookies may affect plugin functionality.
